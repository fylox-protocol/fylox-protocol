What is Fylox?
Fylox is the payment infrastructure layer that Pi Network needs to become a real-world currency.
47 million Pioneers have been holding Pi for years. Fylox gives them a place to spend it — at merchants, between Pioneers, on bills, on flights — without ever converting to fiat.
The goal: A Pioneer wakes up, buys coffee, pays rent, tops up their phone, books a flight — entirely in Pi. No bank account. No exchange. No middlemen.
Five Layers
Layer
Function
ORACLE
Real-world verification tasks — Pioneers earn Pi by confirming events for insurance companies, NGOs, and logistics
NEXUS
On-chain reputation score — KYC-powered trust layer that makes every Pioneer a verified economic actor
AGORA
AI governance certification — companies pay to certify AI models using 47M verified Pioneers as human jury
Integration Marketplace
Local developer integrations — transit cards, utility bills, and services by country
FYLOX
Consumer payment interface — the daily spending layer connecting Pioneers to merchants worldwide

For Pioneers
1. Open Pi Browser on your phone
2. Go to sweet-rugelach-043888.netlify.app
3. Your real Pi balance loads automatically
4. Scan any merchant QR and pay in π
No download. No registration. No bank account.
For Merchants
Fylox provides a unique QR code for each merchant — just like Mercado Pago or MODO, but powered by Pi.
Customer scans QR → enters amount → confirms in π → merchant receives payment
Zero setup cost. Zero transaction fees during beta.
