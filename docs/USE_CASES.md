# Fylox Use Cases

## Overview

Fylox is an experimental payment infrastructure protocol designed to explore how digital currencies can support real-world economic activity.

This document describes potential real-world scenarios where payment infrastructure like Fylox could be useful.

These examples represent possible use cases rather than currently deployed services.

---

# Everyday Retail Payments

One of the most direct applications of a simple payment infrastructure is retail transactions.

Users may scan a QR payment request generated by a merchant and confirm the transaction.

Possible scenarios include:

• grocery stores  
• small shops  
• street vendors  
• local markets  

This interaction allows digital currency to function as a practical payment method in everyday commerce.

---

# Small Merchant Payments

Small businesses often require simple and low-cost payment solutions.

A lightweight QR payment system could allow merchants to receive digital payments without complex infrastructure.

Examples include:

• small restaurants  
• cafés  
• independent stores  
• service providers  

A simple payment flow can lower the barrier for merchant participation.

---

# Public Transportation

Transportation systems often rely on fast and simple payment methods.

In the future, payment infrastructure like Fylox could potentially support:

• bus fares  
• subway access  
• train tickets  
• local transport services  

QR-based transactions may allow quick verification and payment confirmation.

---

# Mobile Services and Utilities

Digital payments could also support everyday services such as:

• mobile phone recharge  
• internet services  
• digital subscriptions  
• basic utilities  

These interactions could allow digital currency to participate in routine financial activity.

---

# Peer-to-Peer Transactions

Individuals may use payment infrastructure for direct transactions with other people.

Possible scenarios include:

• splitting shared expenses  
• paying for informal services  
• local community transactions  

This enables digital currency to circulate within everyday social interactions.

---

# Digital Services and Platforms

Beyond physical commerce, payment infrastructure can also support digital environments.

Potential scenarios include:

• digital marketplaces  
• freelance services  
• online communities  
• creator platforms  

These environments may benefit from simple global payment mechanisms.

---

# Cross-Community Transactions

Digital payment infrastructure can potentially enable transactions between different communities and regions.

This could support:

• international transactions  
• cross-border services  
• global digital marketplaces  

Such interactions may allow digital currency to move beyond local limitations.

---

# Experimental Nature

The use cases described in this document represent exploratory scenarios.

Fylox is currently in early development and focuses on testing simple payment infrastructure.

Real-world implementations may evolve over time as the ecosystem develops.

---

# Long-Term Perspective

The long-term goal of Fylox is to explore how digital currency payment infrastructure can support real economic participation.

By enabling simple interactions between users and merchants, projects like Fylox may contribute to broader experimentation in digital economic systems.
