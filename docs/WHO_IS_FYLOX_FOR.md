# Who Is Fylox For?

## Overview

Fylox is an experimental payment infrastructure protocol designed to explore how digital currencies can support real-world economic activity.

The protocol is intended to serve multiple participants within a digital payment ecosystem.

This document describes the groups that may benefit from payment infrastructure like Fylox.

---

# Developers

Developers are one of the primary audiences for Fylox.

Applications often need simple ways to integrate digital payment capabilities.

Potential developer use cases include:

• marketplaces  
• service platforms  
• local commerce applications  
• digital communities  
• experimental fintech tools  

By providing a structured payment interaction model, Fylox may help developers integrate digital transactions into their applications.

---

# Merchants and Small Businesses

Small businesses frequently require simple and low-cost payment tools.

Potential merchant participants include:

• local shops  
• restaurants and cafés  
• service providers  
• independent retailers  

Payment infrastructure that uses simple QR-based requests may allow businesses to accept digital payments without complex systems.

---

# Digital Platforms

Online platforms may also benefit from integrated digital payments.

Possible participants include:

• online marketplaces  
• freelance platforms  
• creator communities  
• digital service providers  

These environments often require global and flexible payment options.

---

# Communities and Local Economies

Digital payment infrastructure may also support local community activity.

Possible scenarios include:

• community markets  
• neighborhood services  
• local peer-to-peer transactions  

This type of interaction may allow digital currency to circulate within smaller economic networks.

---

# Researchers and Experimenters

Fylox is an experimental project.

Researchers, developers, and entrepreneurs interested in digital economic systems may use the protocol as a platform for experimentation.

Possible areas of exploration include:

• digital payment models  
• community currencies  
• alternative economic participation systems  

---

# Participants in the Pi Ecosystem

Fylox initially explores payment infrastructure within the ecosystem of Pi.

Participants in that ecosystem may experiment with how digital currency can be used in everyday economic interactions.

The project does not replace existing tools but instead explores additional infrastructure possibilities.

---

# Long-Term Perspective

The long-term goal of Fylox is to explore how simple payment infrastructure can support real economic activity.

By serving developers, merchants, platforms, and communities, projects like Fylox may contribute to broader experimentation in digital payment systems.
