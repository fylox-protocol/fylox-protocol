# PRIVACY POLICY

Last updated: 2026

## Introduction

Fylox Protocol is committed to respecting user privacy.

Fylox is designed as an open protocol for digital payments and integrations.  
The protocol itself does not operate centralized user accounts or directly collect personal data.

However, some services, integrations, or interfaces built on top of Fylox may process limited information required for payment interactions.

## Information Collected

The Fylox protocol itself does not store personal user identities.

Depending on how the protocol is used, the following technical information may be processed by applications integrating Fylox:

- Public wallet addresses
- Payment request data (amount, description, reference)
- Transaction metadata
- Merchant identifiers
- QR payment data

This information is generally necessary to execute digital payment interactions.

## Data Storage

Fylox Protocol is designed to minimize centralized data storage.

Transaction data may be recorded on blockchain networks or processed by third-party systems depending on the implementation.

Fylox maintainers do not operate centralized databases of user personal information.

## Third-Party Services

Applications or services that integrate Fylox may use third-party providers such as:

- Wallet applications
- Blockchain networks
- Payment infrastructure
- Analytics tools
- Cloud services

These services operate under their own privacy policies and terms.

Users should review the privacy policies of any application or wallet used with Fylox.

## Security

Fylox Protocol is designed with security and transparency in mind.  
However, no software system can guarantee absolute security.

Users and developers are responsible for implementing proper security practices when integrating or using the protocol.

## User Responsibility

Users are responsible for protecting their wallets, keys, and personal devices.

Fylox Protocol does not manage private keys or custodial accounts.

## Children's Privacy

Fylox Protocol is not designed for or directed at children under the age of 13.

## Regulatory Compliance

Privacy and data protection regulations vary by jurisdiction.

Users, developers, and businesses integrating Fylox are responsible for complying with applicable data protection laws in their region.

## Updates

This privacy policy may be updated as the protocol evolves.
