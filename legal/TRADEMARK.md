# TRADEMARK POLICY

Last updated: 2026

## Overview

"Fylox" and "Fylox Protocol" are project names used to identify the payment protocol and related technologies described in this repository.

The source code of Fylox Protocol may be released under an open-source license (MIT).  
However, the Fylox name, logo, and brand identity may be subject to trademark protection.

This document explains how the Fylox name may be used.

## Acceptable Use

You may use the name "Fylox" or "Fylox Protocol" to:

- Refer to the protocol in educational or informational content
- Discuss the technology in articles, research, or documentation
- Build integrations or tools that interact with the protocol
- Contribute to the open-source development of the project

When possible, please clearly indicate that your project is **independent** from the official Fylox project unless explicit permission has been granted.

Example:

"This project integrates with Fylox Protocol but is not affiliated with the official Fylox project."

## Restricted Use

You may not use the Fylox name, logo, or brand identity in a way that:

- Suggests official endorsement when none exists
- Creates confusion about the source of a product or service
- Impersonates the official Fylox project
- Misleads users or developers

## Commercial Use

Commercial services may integrate with Fylox Protocol.

However, the use of the "Fylox" brand in a commercial product name may require permission from the project maintainers.

Example of acceptable usage:

"Payments powered by Fylox Protocol"

Example of restricted usage without permission:

"Official Fylox Wallet"

## Logo and Branding

Any official Fylox logos or branding assets should not be modified or used in misleading contexts.

Brand assets may be provided separately when available.

## Open Source vs Trademark

The open-source license for the Fylox Protocol codebase does not grant trademark rights to the Fylox name or branding.

Open-source code and trademark rights are separate legal concepts.

## Contact

For trademark-related questions or permission requests, please contact the project maintainers through the official repository.

## Updates

This trademark policy may be updated as the project evolves.
